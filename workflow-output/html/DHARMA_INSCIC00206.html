Censer (dhūpādhāra) Censer (dhūpādhāra)

Metadata

Identifier:

INSCIC00206

Hands:

First edition of the file: First digital edition made by École française d’Extrême-Orient (EFEO), realized in collaboration with the Institute for the Study of the Ancient World (ISAW) at New York University as The Corpus of the Inscriptions of Campā, in 2010-2012.

Edition

1tam imañ ca dhūpādhāraṃ śrīrudravarmmadevo dattavān iti |||

Apparatus

1 tam imañ ca dhūpādhāraṃ ◇ [ta mi ma smam] sahādhāraḥ • There is clearly no visarga but rather the expected anusvāra. — 1 ||| • The punctuation is not represented by Schweyer.

Translation by Arlo Griffiths

And Śrī Rudravarmadeva has given this censer here.

Translation into French by Arlo Griffiths

Et Śrī Rudravarmadeva a donné ce porte-encens-ci.
