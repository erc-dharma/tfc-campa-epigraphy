    Block at Mỹ Sơn

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-06-20Z

    First edition of the file:

    First digital edition made by École française d’Extrême-Orient (EFEO), realized in collaboration with the Institute for the Study of the Ancient World (ISAW) at New York University as The Corpus of the Inscriptions of Campā, in 2010-2012.

    Editors:   Arlo Griffths and Salomé Pichon.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIC00075

    Hand Description:

    Additional Metadata

    Edition

    1 || di śakarāja 913 huriy· 5 vaṅun· vulān· 4 vr̥haspativāra (ma)-
    2ghanakṣatra vr̥ścikalagna kāla yāṅ· po ku vijaya śrī harivarmmadeva
    3punaḥ yāṅ· po ku śrī jaya īśānabhadreśvara karaṇa kīrtti yaśa di bhūmima-
    4ṇḍala niy· || fleuron

    Apparatus

    1 913 ◇ 917 KHG; 713 LF • The reading 7 for the units that Golzio proposes is impossible, as inspection of the estampage shows. See our discussion in Griffiths et al. 2008–2009: page 486. — 1vulān· 4 LF ◇ vulān 8 (?) LF • Finot first read the month name as 8 (?). We adopt the reading that he later proposed with confidence (Finot 1904: page 933).

    Translation by Arlo Griffiths

    In the [year of the] Śaka-king 913, day 5 of the waxing [fortnight] of month 4, Thursday, lunar mansion Magha, Scorpio in the ascendant. [This was] the time that Y.P.K. the victorious Śrī Harivarmadeva re-established (the image of) Y.P.K. Śrī Jaya Īśānabhadreśvara, for the sake of fame and glory in this earthly domain.

    Translation into French by & al 2008-2009

    En [l’année] 913 [du] roi des Śaka, jour 5 de la [quinzaine] croissante du mois 4, un jeudi, mansion lunaire de Maghā, l’ascendant en Scorpion. [Ceci fut] le temps où Y. P. K. le victorieux Śrī Harivarmadeva rétablit (l’image de) Y. P. K. Śrī Jaya Īśānabhadreśvara, en vue de la gloire et de la renommée dans ce domaine terrestre.

    Commentary

    On the issue of the correct reading and interpretation of the dating formula in this inscription, see Griffiths et al. 2008–2009: page 457.
