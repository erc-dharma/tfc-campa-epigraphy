Translation by Salomé Pichon

A1-A3In this [year] 897 snaḥ: the man who offers fields at that time, the pu lyaṅ Śrī Jayasiṅhavarma, along with Pulki Kumāramātya kluñ (chief) of the city, pom̃ ku kluñ Kunurun, Satyādhipatiḥ, pom̃ Saṅgrāmacar, Sabhādharmmādhikār, pom̃ Jayavikraṁ, pom̃ Yudhādhikār, pom̃ ku Nyāyādhipatiḥ, Raṁgyāv Hrey Atāl, Atuk Anāk pom̃ kluñ Jrāy and pvaḥ (novice) Dat.
