Rock of Đông Yên Châu (C. 174)

2kuruN ko jmāY labuḥ nari svarggaḥ ya UrāṄ paribhū d(i) ko

Apparatus

1 nī ◇ ni GCCoedes 1939 — 1 yāṄ ◇ yaṄ GCCoedes 1939

Translation

1-3Success! This is the divine serpent of the king. People who are gentle to this lord (ko kurun), they (ko) shall not fall from heaven. People who insult this lord, a thousand years they suffer from fever in hell with their seven generations of descendants.

Translation into French by Coedes (1937-1966)

1-3Succès ! Ceci est le saint nāga du roi. Quiconque le traite doucement....... des joyaux tombent ciel ; quiconque l’insulte...... pendant 1000 années [il souffre] aux enfers avec sept générations de sa famille.
