B1 Utkr̥ṣṭarāja kā ◇ Utkr̥ṣṭarāja parisamāpta FFinot 1904
B2 lyaṅ· • The end of the line is not clear, but it would seem that what looks like a r is a virama whose shape isn’t identical as those read previously.
B3 mahāsenāpatī • The ā of mahā isn’t the usual vertical stroke placed after the consonant, but it is written above the h, in a spiral shape. Later addition to the engraving of the text? Not a single case, cf. Svayamutpanna inscriptions. — B3 sahodara • Same remark as for the ā of mahā (for the o). Might be read sarahādara, but doesn’t seem to mean anything.
B4 Anekaguṇasaṁpanna ◇ Anekaguṇa guṇasaṁpanna FFinot 1904

The reading of the akṣara na in sthāna is not clear, I read ḥ. — B8 liṅyak· ◇ liṅgyak· FFinot 1904 • It must be a typo.
