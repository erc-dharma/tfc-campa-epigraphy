    Rock at Lệ Cam

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2024-02-14Z

    First edition of the file:

    First digital edition made by École française d’Extrême-Orient (EFEO), realized in collaboration with the Institute for the Study of the Ancient World (ISAW) at New York University as The Corpus of the Inscriptions of Campā, in 2010-2012.

    Editors:   Arlo Griffths and Salomé Pichon.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSCIC00127

    Hand Description:

    Additional Metadata

    No metadata were provided in the table for this inscription

    Edition

    C. 127.1

    1 namaś śivāya
    2 śrīprakāśadharmmā jaya(ti)

    C. 127.2

    1 śrīcampeśvara

    Apparatus

    C. 127.1

    2jaya(ti) ◇ jayadā[naṁ] • This reading was repeated, without square brackets and introducing a new variation (prakāśavarmmā), in Schweyer 2009: pages 35, note 60.

    Translation

    C. 127.1

    Homage to Śiva. Śrī Prakāśadharmmā triumphs!

    C. 127.2

    Lord of Campā.

    Translation into French

    C. 127.1

    Hommage à Śiva. Śrī Prakāśadharmmā triomphe !

    C. 127.2

    Seigneur du Campā.

    Bibliography

    Translated in Finot 1915: page 112; whence Majumdar 1985: page 28 and Golzio 2004: page 12. The present edition and translation after Griffiths et al. 2008–2009: pages 463–464.
