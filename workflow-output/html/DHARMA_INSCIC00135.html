Translation

The illustrious conqueror, king of Campā, of great wisdom, called Śrī Prakāśadharman, has established Amareśa, the great...

Translation into French

L’illustre conquérant, roi du Campā, de grande sagesse, appelé Śrī Prakāśadharman, a établi Amareśa, le grand...

Commentary

It was not recognized by Huber that the inscription is metrical. Insufficient materials are at our disposal at this time to establish the whole text, but what is clear is that it concerns the erection of Amareśa, no doubt a liṅga of Śiva. Cf. Bisschop 2006: page 33 with n. 79

Bibliography

First published, with French translation, in Huber 1911: page 261; whence, with English translation, in Majumdar 1985: page 15, whence Golzio 2004: page 10 Re-edited from the inked EFEO estampages and available photos, with new translation, in Goodall and Griffiths 2013: pages 427–428, whence the present edition.
